Chipotle Adobo Shrimp

Ingredients:

  • 7oz Chipotles in Adobo Sauce*
  • Shrimp (I used Red Argentinian Shrimp)
  • Cumin powder
  • Chili powder
  • Onion powder
  • Garlic powder
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt
  • Pickled Red Onion
  • Slaw
  • Fresh cilantro
  • Carrot Slivers
  • Romaine
  • Oil
  • Tortillas

Instructions:

  1. Make the slaw. Cut carrots into slivers. Thinly slice romaine. Add carrots to a bowl with pickled red onions and romaine. Drizzle a tiny bit of olive oil.
  2. Heat a sauce pan on medium. Cook the shrimp on each side. When flipping the shrimp to the second side, add in as much sauce as you'd like *See notes about spice level.
  3. Optional: heat tortillas in a second sauce pan.
  4. Assemble the tortillas with shrimp, and top with slaw.
  5. Serve immediately with a garnish with cilantro.

Notes:

  • We used almost all the adobo sauce and found the dish to be spicy, in a good way! If you do not like as much spice, I suggest using half or less.
  • I used Red Argentinian Shrimp from Costco. This is a delicate shrimp. I suggest thawing it gently. Keep in mind that it cooks fast.
